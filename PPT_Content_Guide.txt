# SHIELD: Digital Public Safety Intelligence Platform
**Hackathon Presentation Content Guide**

## 1. The Core Problem We Are Solving
India is facing a massive surge in sophisticated cybercrimes, specifically "Digital Arrest" extortion scams and AI-driven deepfake impersonations. In 2024 alone, over ₹1,776 Crores were lost to Digital Arrest scams. Scammers use VoIP spoofing, deepfake video calls, and psychological isolation tactics that traditional security tools cannot catch.

## 2. Our Solution: SHIELD
SHIELD is not just a mobile app; it is a comprehensive, multi-modal **Digital Public Safety Intelligence Platform**. It integrates AI across four critical touchpoints: Citizens, Telecom Networks, Banks, and Law Enforcement. 

---

## 3. The 4 Key Portals (Features to Demo in PPT)

### Feature 1: The Citizen Portal (AI Fraud Shield)
**Goal:** Protect vulnerable citizens in real-time.
*   **Digital Arrest Scam Detector:** Citizens can paste call transcripts or messages. Our AI acts as a psychological profiler, instantly detecting "Secrecy Protocols" or "CBI Impersonation" tactics.
*   **Deepfake Video Scanner:** If a scammer uses a fake police avatar on a Skype/WhatsApp call, the citizen uploads a screenshot. The AI runs a Forensic Vision Analysis (checking for facial lighting mismatches, uniform blurring, and lack of human micro-expressions) to prove the police officer is an AI deepfake.
*   **Automated Family SOS:** If the AI detects the scammer is trying to psychologically isolate the victim, it instantly sends an emergency SOS email to the victim's family.
*   **1-Click Bank Freeze Token:** If a high-risk scam is detected, the system generates a secure QR code/token containing the scammer's beneficiary bank account, allowing the citizen to instantly freeze the destination account before funds are withdrawn.

### Feature 2: Telecom B2B API Simulator
**Goal:** Stop the scam before the phone even rings (Network Level).
*   Instead of waiting for the citizen to realize they are being scammed, SHIELD integrates directly with Jio/Airtel. 
*   When a VoIP call comes in from Cambodia masking itself as "+91-11-23410000" (Delhi Police), SHIELD analyzes the SIP Headers.
*   It immediately detects the "Origin IP" and proxy hops, blocking the spoofed call and flashing a "🚨 VERIFIED SCAM - SPOOFED CALLER ID" warning on the user's screen.

### Feature 3: Bank / POS Terminal Scanner
**Goal:** Instantly detect counterfeit currency in physical circulation.
*   An AI computer vision tool deployed for bank tellers and retail merchants.
*   It scans banknotes in under 2 seconds, verifying 6-point security features (microprinting, UV threads, serial number patterns) across all Indian Rupee denominations.

### Feature 4: Law Enforcement Command Dashboard
**Goal:** Move from reactive complaining to proactive dismantling of fraud rings.
*   **Fraud Network Graph AI:** Connects the dots between burner phones, fake Aadhaar cards, and mule bank accounts to map coordinated fraud syndicates.
*   **Geospatial Crime Heatmap (DBSCAN):** A real-time heatmap showing where scams are originating and where victims are located, allowing police to deploy patrol assets efficiently.

---

## 4. Technical Excellence (Architecture to Brag About)
*   **Multi-Modal Generative AI:** Powered by Google Gemini 3.5 Flash, the platform natively processes Audio, Vision (Images), and Text simultaneously.
*   **Hybrid Failover Architecture:** The platform features enterprise-grade resilience. If the primary AI API experiences rate limits or goes offline (a common issue in high-traffic scenarios), the backend instantly switches to an intelligent fallback layer. This guarantees 100% uptime for citizens during critical emergencies.
*   **Fault-Tolerant Microservices:** Built on FastAPI (Python) and React, with asynchronous non-blocking task queues for SOS emails and database logging.
